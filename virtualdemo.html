Benefits of Joining The Local Cup

Adopting The Local Cup's mobile ordering app helps small coffee shops compete with larger chains and meet the changing expectations of their customers.

Coffee shops that implement mobile ordering see a range of benefits, from increased efficiency and convenience to improved customer loyalty and engagement.

Many coffee shops are already experienceing the convenience and benefits of improved operations and customer experience by introducing mobile ordering through The Local Cup app.

Increased efficiency.

The Local Cup app can streamline the ordering process and reduce the time it takes for customers to place an order.

Mobile ordering can help shorten lines and wait times for customers.

Additionally, mobile ordering means cashless purchasing, another way to streamline the process.

Improved accuracy.

With The Local Cup app, customers can enter their orders directly into the system, reducing the chances of errors caused by miscommunication or misinterpretation of orders.

Customers are able to customize and confirm their order themselves when mobile ordering.

Marketing opportunities.

The Local Cup app can provide small coffee shops with a platform to promote their products and services.

Member coffee shops are able to set specials, offer deals, and more.

Member coffee shops are able to send messages via push notifications and text messages to their customers.

Cost-effective.

Outsourcing mobile ordering to The Local Cup app can be more cost-effective than developing an app in-house.

By joining The Local Cup, you can free up your internal resources to focus on your core business activities.

Additionally, joining The Local Cup can enable your customers to start ordering via mobile within a few weeks.
